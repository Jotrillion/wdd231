For the BourseNetwork

This platform aims to connect students with scholarship opportunities worldwide and recommendation information along with a membership information form.

Site Purpose BourseNetwork serves as a global gateway connecting prospective students with a diverse range of worldwide scholarship and funding opportunities. The platform will provide: A searchable and categorized database of scholarship opportunities from various countries and institutions. Curated recommendation information to guide students through the application process (e.g., essay tips, required documents, deadlines). A secure membership information form for users to register, personalize their scholarship search, and receive tailored updates

Target Audience Scenario Questions The target audience for BourseNetwork consists primarily of students—from high school to postgraduate levels—seeking financial aid for their education. Scenario 1 (Undergraduate Seeker): "I am a high school student in Ghana who wants to study Engineering in Canada next fall. I have a strong academic record, but my parents cannot afford international tuition. Where on BourseNetwork can I filter scholarships specifically for Ghanaian students pursuing STEM in North America, and what is the maximum number of scholarships I can save to my personal dashboard?" Scenario 2 (Postgraduate/Recommendation Focus): "I am preparing to apply for a Master's program in Public Health in the UK. One of the top scholarships requires three letters of recommendation. Where can I find sample recommendation letter formats, and does BourseNetwork offer a checklist or guide on the best way to approach professors for strong recommendations?"
